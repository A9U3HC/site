more


i paused just for a moment

not even a second

my attention deviated from the mundane television show

to the constant low drum of rain drops falling on the roof

and i thought

i want to do something

more

learn something more

for me

not for them

she rested her face on my shoulder

and we shared a moment

as we stretched our bodies

at the same time

she pulled closer to me and fell asleep

i want more
